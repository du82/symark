How to use SyMark

Created on May 6th, 2025, updated on June 12th, 2025

SyMark is easy to use, just download the binary for your platform, export your SiYuan notebook as a .sy.zip​ file and extract its contents into the input​ folder of SyMark. Run the program and your resulting webpages will appear in the output​ folder!
